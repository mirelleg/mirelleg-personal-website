Welcome to Bakers' Block!

Seeing my mama in the kitchen baking desserts as a kid, I knew that I wanted to be like her, a talented baker, in the future. As a baker for over 10 years, I started with pre-made cake and cookie mixes and transitioned to baking from scratch. There truly is nothing like homemade desserts.

Bakers' Block is my space to share some of the best recipes I have used to create delightful desserts. Hope you try them out and enjoy them just as much as I did!
